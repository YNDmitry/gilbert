History

John Gilbert and Company was established in 1932 by Johns great grandfather, John Henry Gilbert, NZ.

The company began with small goods and grocery supplies evolving into radio electronics into door gear, roof hatch and window systems for public transport vechiles evolving into residential home construction.

We’re looking to evoke feelings of modular architecturally designed homes that are inspiring, yet made mindfully. Designed and built for the style concious person with functionality in mind.

Being a family fun business for over 80 years we need to hold  onto that tradition, yet attacking it with a modern and new age approach to appeal to young people looking for quality homes at an affordable price.

Our name and brand will need to create a sense of trust and respect while also having a unique look that will disrupt the market.
